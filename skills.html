USER RESEARCH

My approach to user research is to let the user do as much talking as they can. By asking open ended questions, you can learn more about who is using your product and get the chase to ask follow up questions. While it’s great to meet interviewees in person, I have experience with Zoom and video conferencing.

USER EMPATHY

It’s important to think of yourself in the user’s shoes. When creating a usable interface, I try to remember to take a step out of my own point of view and remember the primary user of the product.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

A good interview is one where you take time to listen to consumer, but also know when you are off topic. While following up to get more information is important, it is equally important to remember that we are trying to gather information.
